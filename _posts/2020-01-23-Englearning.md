### I think:

1. I am not going to lie/ I'm not gonna lie/ Not gonna lie/  NGL
2. I  all honesty(informal)
3. In my view
4. If you ask me, ...
5. As far as I can tell (Informal/formal)
6. To my mind (formal)
7. As far as I'm consider  (slightly more formal)
8. The way I see things/it ...
9. As I see things/it ...
10. It seem to me that (也是比较中性的，可能偏正式一点)
11. I believe
12. I would/ I'd say
13. I consider... to be ...
14. To me ...
15. From my point of view.. (Slightly more formal, but can be used in both informal and formal  situations.)
16. It is my view/belief that ...
17. I reckon... (British one): I reckon the sixth gin and tonic tipped me over the edge. 我认为第六杯金汤力让我上头了。
18.  I honestly believe that...
19. Honestly speaking (informal)
20. I feel that...
21. Personally speaking (中性，正式和非正式均可)

### Response to thank u

1. You are very welcome.
2. No problem, I am enjoy to help you.
3. No, thank you.
4. The pleasure is mine! (formal)/ My pleasure/Pleasure.(informal)
5. 熟人：I know you'd do the same for me!
6. (casual) That's alright. (别人以为你帮他做的事情非愿意。但这个也可以非常warm，取决于说这句话的语气)
7. (very casual one) No worries!
8. (British) Don't mention it!
9. (Very heartful, very self-deprecating) It was the least I could do.
10. Anytime. (very casual)
11. (very USA one) Sure, no problem./ Sure.
12. It was nothing.
13. (Business situation, More formal) Much obliged！/I am very much obliged to you.这句old fashion，可用于商店啥的。
14. (Business situation, More formal)  You are most welcome.
